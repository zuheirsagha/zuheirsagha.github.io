The Challenge

Popcorn Priests provides their customers with a healthy alternative to snacking. Their main product consists of freshly popped popcorn coated in a gourmet whey protein chocolate syrup. This means you can indulge in a sweet snack while staying healthy.

My goal was to design a company logo, business card and packaging.

Logo

The logo is inspired by the raw ingredients in their flagship product, popcorn and chocolate.

The crown is placed on the kernel as a fun twist that helps to further solidify the gourmet appearance of the product.

Business Card

For the business card design I wanted to create a minimal card that clearly showed all necessary information with the brand name clearly labelled in multiple locations.

The blue background offers a nice contrast to the brown chocolate coating on the kernal.
